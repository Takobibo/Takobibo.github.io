GAME RULES
Catch the correct falling products according to the phase.
Phase 1 (0-20s): ManMilk
Phase 2 (20-40s): Good Wife's Jam
Phase 3 (40-60s): Ego Eggs
Use A/D or Arrow Keys to move left/right.
Avoid catching wrong items — game ends immediately.
Survive 60 seconds to win!
Double Challenge Level 2:
Press SPACE to jump (double jump allowed).
Jump over gaps and catch floating products.
Falling into a gap ends the game.
