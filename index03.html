Event Info

SUPERGO encourages all participants to take on the "Fridge Landscape Challenge"! Decorate your refrigerator with SUPERGO products and showcase your unique creativity.

How to participate:
1. Fill your fridge with SUPERGO products (any combination of our items).
2. Take a high-quality photo of your decorated fridge interior.
3. Upload the photo to our official website or social media with the hashtag #SUPERGOFridgeChallenge.

Selection & Prizes:
A panel of SUPERGO designers and lifestyle experts will select four winners based on creativity, aesthetic appeal, and originality.
Grand Prize (1 winner): A year's supply of SUPERGO products + $500 gift card.
Excellence Awards (3 winners): $200 gift card each + a SUPERGO mystery box.

Additional perks for all participants:
Every valid entry receives a 10% discount code for your next SUPERGO purchase.
Top 20 finalists will have their fridge landscapes featured on our official social media channels.

Judging criteria:
- Creativity and originality (40%)
- Visual appeal and composition (30%)
- Effective use of SUPERGO products (20%)
- Storytelling / theme (10%)

Important dates:
Submission opens: April 1, 2025
Submission deadline: December 31, 2025
Finalist announcement: January 15, 2026
Winners announced: January 30, 2026

Terms & conditions:
By participating, you grant SUPERGO the right to use your submitted images for promotional purposes. Winners will be notified via email. Prizes are non-transferable. SUPERGO reserves the right to disqualify any entry that violates our community guidelines.

Submission deadline: December 31, 2025. Winners will be announced on our official channels within 30 days after the deadline. Don't miss this chance to turn your fridge into a masterpiece – and win amazing prizes!
